# EIN / IRS Process Notes

## Who to call
IRS Business & Specialty Tax Line: +1 800-829-4933 (Mon–Fri 07:00–19:00 local).
General IRS assistance: +1 800-829-1040.

## What to say for a foreign-owner LLC
- Request: 'I'd like to apply for an EIN for a single-member LLC.'
- Form: SS-4; foreign owners cannot e-file, must fax/phone.
- Responsible Party: sole member's full legal name + ITIN or 'Foreign' with passport.
- Reason: 'Started a new business' or 'Banking purposes'.

## Gotchas
- IVR loop: press 1 (English), then 1, then 3, then 2 to reach EIN unit.
- Hold times 30–90 min typical.
- Agent will verbally assign EIN; write it down + request fax confirmation (Form 147C).
